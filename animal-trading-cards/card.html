Human

Humans are the only animals known for putting a lot of effort building technology that would do what they can already do by themselves.

  • Scientific Name: Homo Sapiens Sapiens
  • Average Height: 150-190 cm
  • Average Weight: 45-100 kg
  • Habitat: The entire world

Humans are very curious animals, with very peculiar habits. They distinguish themselves from all other animals for taking selfies and, among the other things, for gossiping about other humans but complaining when other humans gossip about them. They are the only animals that need to earn money and spend it to be able to survive. They also spend money in many other creative ways: for water, for speaking with one another over the phone, or for paying taxes to their own countries.
